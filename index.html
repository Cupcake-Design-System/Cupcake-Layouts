Quickview Basic Setup
Quickview is basically just a right sidebar. The name "c-quickview" is to hopefully help avoid confusion with existing sidebars, sidenav etc.

It ONLY shows on top of the content. There is no push option that moves the content. That functionality is saved for the aside.

This shows the usage of .quickview-header and .quickview-footer with a few media objects in the conten area.
We also have additional options for sizing and backdrops.
Aside Basic Setup
Asides are for internal page navigation, list groups etc.

It can live on the left or right of the content.
We also have additional options for sizing and backdrops.
